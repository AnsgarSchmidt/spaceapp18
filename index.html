The mission

For the last 10 years NASA hosted a global and one of the biggest hackathons world wide. With over 25.000 in 69 countries this event is attracting tallents from technology, art, culture, science, engineering and health.

The Berlin Hackathon is a 30 hour event where participants work cooperatively on solution for the future. Students, employies, startups and highly motivated people design, prototype, build and present results within one weekend. In a creative and playfull environment visions become craspable and real.

We are hosted by the Motionlab in Berlin. One of the biggest Makerspaces in the world where we have access to modern tools from 3D printers to laser cutters and CNC mills. Beside the environment we are also supported by a group of highly tallented and creative mentors.
